The Nawabs From North

Curated Royal Cuisine from the Kitchens of the North

Our Story

Inspired by the regal kitchens of Awadh, Punjab, Kashmir, Bengal and the legendary Grand Trunk Road, every dish is a celebration of timeless recipes, slow cooking, hand-ground spices and uncompromising craftsmanship. We invite you to experience the grandeur of India's culinary heritage, one exquisite plate at a time.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

What Makes Every Plate Royal

Traditional Techniques

Slow-cooked handi curries, dum-sealed biryanis and charcoal tandoor grilling — methods passed down through generations of royal kitchens.

Hand-Ground Spices

Every masala is ground fresh in-house, never store-bought, so each dish carries the full depth of its spices.

Fresh, Never Frozen

Every dish is prepared fresh to order using premium ingredients — nothing pre-made, nothing reheated.

A Dish, Remembered

We hope your meal is not merely enjoyed, but remembered — a small taste of the grandeur of Hindustan's royal kitchens.
